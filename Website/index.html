Our Story:

Dave Montgomerie and Jenny Plane both grew up on farms in the Bay of Plenty's own Te Puke. Both went off to the university of Auckland to study logistics and commerce respectively where they meet international student Wei Chan an expert on fruit and veges. They got talking and decided that together with their talents they could grow fruit and veges for all NZ to enjoy thus Terra's Garden was born.
